So, who are we

We are an independent coffee house in the center of Salisbury, and we are passionate about pure coffee experience.

What else can we offer you

Besides the perfectly crafted coffees we are proud about, there is also a great selection of wines and freshly-made foods - from sandwiches to pastries, cakes and waffles, all made daily in our kitchen for freshness.

If you're in a hurry, we thought about that too, so it can be worth choosing our takeaway service.

You may consult our products list on the MENU dedicated page.

How’s the location

Our location has a distinct modern design, with a warming atmosphere. The windows are looking right at the Avon River and you can admire a family of ducks paddling on the water or the swans flock, while they are guarding and rearing their offspring.

The venue is divided in multiple rooms, creating a feeling of intimacy for friends coming together, families with kids or anyone else who wants to let their guard down and relax until it feels like home.

But also, our back Secret Garden, when weather is kind to us, is remarkable for enjoying your coffee in the morning or in the afternoon.

Sounds incredible and want to have a look at some pictures taken in our café? We have that ready for you in the GALLERY section.

Where can you find us

If you'd like to visit us, to call us directly or read clients' reviews on our social media pages, please check at the bottom of the page, where you'll find all the information necessary to do so.

Is it suitable for private hire

Yes, of course, we also host events, so please ask if you're part of a group or would like to book our space.

While we've covered some introductory topics, if there is any other question, please visit our CONTACT page and fill the provided form. We will look into this and get back to you as soon as possible.


Thank you and looking forward to meet you in person and to serve you with the best coffee in the area!
